Deceptive vs Ethical Design

The same cookie consent dialog, designed two different ways.

⚠ Deceptive Design

We use cookies to improve your experience.

Manage preferences (may affect site experience)
1. Accept button is large and colorful; reject is hidden as a tiny link.
2. Wording implies rejecting will "affect experience" to scare users.
3. No granular control - all or nothing.
✓ Ethical Design

We use cookies. You can choose which to allow.

Essential
Analytics
Marketing
1. Each cookie type can be controlled individually.
2. Both buttons have equal visual weight - respects user choice.
3. Transparent about why essential cookies stay on.
